--- title: "Sleepy wanderings" date: 2018-05-05 ---

A bit of a sleepy day with some walking around the city. A bit limited today due to digestive concerns, but Emma and I took care of some errands and found a local gym. Found an aqueduct, though!

Águas Livres Aqueduct

That night, we went out to watch an intratown soccer rivalry between the two local teams. The restaurant bar owner was very accomodating, moving a table basically inside the entryway to let us see the television, and shooing away other patrons when they blocked our view. Lisbon is just an enjoyable city to wander around - tiles (azulejos) everywhere!
